## Overview

Krisp is a Voice AI platform that makes meetings clearer and more productive by running on a unified AI voice engine. Founded in 2017 for remote workers and call centers, Krisp has evolved into a comprehensive meeting assistant with noise cancellation, transcription, and AI note-taking capabilities.

## Features

### Real-Time Voice Processing
- **AI Noise Cancellation** - #1 noise cancellation that removes background sounds (dogs barking, keyboard clicks, traffic, fans) without affecting voice quality
- **Accent Conversion** - Convert accents for both speakers and listeners to improve comprehension
- **Voice Translation** - Real-time translation for multilingual calls
- **Echo Removal** - Eliminate echo and cross-talk for crystal-clear audio
- **Works Everywhere** - Compatible with every conferencing app (Zoom, Teams, Meet, Webex, Slack)

### Meeting Automation
- **Automatic Transcription** - Real-time transcription in 16 languages
- **AI Meeting Summaries** - Automatically generated meeting notes and summaries
- **Action Item Tracking** - Extract and track action items across sessions
- **Meeting Recording** - Record in audio and video modes
- **Custom Vocabulary** - Support for up to 750 custom terms and jargon

### Integration & Workflow
- **CRM Synchronization** - Integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Slack
- **Chrome Extension** - Browser-based meeting support
- **Mobile Apps** - iOS and Android apps for mobile meetings
- **Platform Agnostic** - Works at the audio level with any conferencing tool
