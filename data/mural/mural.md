## Overview

Mural is a collaboration platform that helps teams capture, organize, and act on ideas through visual collaboration. Designed with structured facilitation and design thinking methodologies at its core, Mural provides an AI-powered workspace where remote, in-person, and asynchronous teams can work together effectively.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Mind Maps**: Automatically generate mind maps from brainstorming sessions
- **Cluster & Classify**: Organize research and ideas with AI-assisted clustering and categorization
- **Facilitation Superpowers**: Built-in timers, voting, anonymous contributions, and structured workshop flows
- **Method Guides**: Pre-built frameworks for design thinking, lean, agile, and other collaborative methodologies
- **Template Library**: Extensive collection of pre-designed templates for workshops, retrospectives, and planning sessions
- **Multiple Work Modes**: Support for in-person, remote, and asynchronous collaboration seamlessly
- **Microsoft 365 Integration**: Native integration with Microsoft Teams, Outlook, and Azure DevOps
- **Copilot Integration**: Microsoft 365 Copilot integration for AI-assisted workshop facilitation
- **Org Chart & Mapping**: Create organizational structures and customer journey maps
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Works on web, mobile, and Microsoft Teams
