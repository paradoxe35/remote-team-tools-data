## Overview

Hyperise is a video creation tool that allows users to generate personalized video messages at scale. For remote teams, it enables authentic one-to-one communication for employee onboarding, team updates, and personal outreach without requiring video production expertise.

## Key Features

- **Personalized Videos**: Generate videos with personalized text, images, and messaging
- **Template Library**: Pre-designed templates for various use cases
- **Video Editor**: Intuitive editor for customizing videos with text, stickers, and effects
- **Brand Kit**: Apply brand colors, logos, and fonts to maintain consistency
- **Avatar Support**: Use digital avatars or record yourself for personalized messages
- **Bulk Creation**: Automatically create personalized videos for multiple recipients
- **Email Integration**: Generate unique video links and embed in emails
- **Analytics**: Track video views, engagement, and completion rates
- **Screen Recording**: Record and annotate screen captures for tutorials and demos
- **Library Management**: Organize and manage video templates and assets
- **Export Options**: Download videos in various formats or get shareable links
- **API Access**: Programmatically create videos through API integration

## Pricing

- **Free**: Basic video creation with limited features and Hyperise branding
- **Pro**: $25/month with more template options and advanced analytics
- **Business**: $99/month with team features, bulk creation, and integrations
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for large-scale deployments and API access
