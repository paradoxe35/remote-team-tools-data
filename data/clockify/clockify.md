## Overview

Clockify is a free time tracking software designed for remote teams and freelancers. It allows users to track time spent on tasks, generate detailed reports, and manage projects efficiently.

## Features

- **Free Tier**: Unlimited time tracking for individuals and teams
- **Timer Interface**: Start/stop tracking with a click
- **Project & Task Management**: Organize time entries by projects and tasks
- **Detailed Reports**: Export time logs and generate productivity insights
- **Team Management**: Track multiple team members' time
- **Integrations**: Works with Jira, Trello, Asana, and other tools

## Pricing

- **Free Tier**: All essential features for unlimited users
- **Premium (Business)**: $7.50/user/month - Advanced reports and team analytics
- **Advanced (Team Leaders)**: $12.50/user/month - Custom fields and bulk edits
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for large organizations
