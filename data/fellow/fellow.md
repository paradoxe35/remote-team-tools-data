## Overview

Fellow is an AI-powered meeting minutes and notetaker application that transforms conversations into searchable, shareable meeting records. It works across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Slack huddles to capture discussion points, decisions, and action items automatically.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Recording & Transcription**: Records and transcribes meetings across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Slack huddles with human-level accuracy
- **AI Summarization**: Intelligent summaries that capture key discussion points and decisions
- **Action Item Extraction**: Automatically identifies and organizes follow-up tasks from meeting content
- **Meeting Minutes Templates**: Purpose-built templates for different meeting types (sales calls, 1-on-1s, project syncs, interviews, all-hands)
- **AskFellow AI Agent**: AI-powered tool for asking questions about meeting content and generating follow-up documents
- **Document Automation**: Generate emails, memos, and CRM updates automatically from meeting notes
- **Meeting Search**: Make all meeting transcriptions searchable and easily accessible
- **Integrations**: Salesforce, HubSpot, Monday, Asana, ClickUp, Linear, Jira, Notion, Confluence, Slack, Zapier, and others
- **Privacy-First**: Fellow never trains on your data; uses enterprise-grade encryption and SOC 2-compliant infrastructure
- **No Screenshots**: Privacy-respecting approach without screen recording or surveillance

## Security & Privacy

- **Enterprise-Grade**: SOC 2-compliant infrastructure with strict access controls
- **Data Protection**: Encrypted storage and no use of customer data for model training
- **Compliance**: Designed for sensitive conversations and regulated industries
