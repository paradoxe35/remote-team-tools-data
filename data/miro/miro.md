## Overview

Miro is an online whiteboard platform built around an infinite canvas that excels at real-time visual collaboration. It enables remote teams to brainstorm, plan, design, and map ideas together, supporting both synchronous and asynchronous work across distributed teams.

## Key Features

- **Infinite Canvas**: Unlimited workspace for unlimited creativity with zoomable and pannable interface
- **5,000+ Templates**: Pre-built templates for agile planning, design thinking, strategy workshops, product roadmaps, and more
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Live cursors, comments, and reactions with presence indicators for simultaneous editing
- **Facilitation Tools**: Built-in timers, voting, anonymous sticky notes, and breakout rooms for workshops
- **AI Features**: AI-powered summarization, clustering, and diagram generation from unstructured ideas
- **160+ Integrations**: Seamless connection with Slack, Jira, Confluence, Microsoft Teams, Azure DevOps, and other tools
- **Multiple Views**: Whiteboard canvas, diagramming, process mapping, and org charting capabilities
- **Asynchronous Support**: Comment threads, reactions, and persistent changes enable async collaboration across time zones
- **Smart Shapes & Connectors**: Pre-built diagram elements and intelligent connectors for flowcharts and technical drawings
