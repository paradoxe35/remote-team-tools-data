## Overview

Miro is a visual collaboration platform that provides an infinite digital canvas where remote teams can collaborate on brainstorming, planning, design, and process mapping. It supports synchronous and asynchronous teamwork.

## Key Features

- **Infinite Canvas**: Unlimited space for brainstorming and planning
- **Diagramming Tools**: Shapes, connectors, and drawing tools
- **Templates**: Pre-built templates for common workflows (mind maps, flowcharts, wireframes)
- **Real-Time Collaboration**: Work together with team members simultaneously
- **Sticky Notes**: Digital sticky notes for ideation
- **Integrations**: Connect with Slack, Microsoft Teams, Jira, and other tools
- **Presentations**: Present your work directly from the board
- **Voting & Commenting**: Gather feedback from team members
- **Mobile Support**: Work on iOS and Android
- **Permissions**: Manage access and collaboration levels
- **API**: Programmatic access for custom integrations

## Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Basic whiteboarding with limited features
- **Team**: $16 per user/month (billed annually), includes unlimited boards and viewers
- **Business**: $30 per user/month, adds advanced security and team management
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing with dedicated support and advanced compliance features
