## Overview

Claap is a comprehensive AI platform designed to automate sales workflows and provide intelligence for sales teams. The platform offers visibility on every sales call with no manual note-taking, using AI agents that handle administrative tasks automatically. Trusted by over 1,000 companies including Qonto, Revolut, and 360Learning.

## Features

### AI Agent
- **Meeting Recording & Transcription** - Automatically records and transcribes meetings in 100+ languages
- **AI Note-Taking** - Generates personalized summaries using customizable templates
- **Email Generation** - Creates follow-up emails that match your communication style
- **CRM Auto-Enrichment** - Automatically updates CRM fields with relevant deal information

### AI Analyst
- **Pipeline Intelligence** - Explores sales pipeline to identify deal patterns and opportunities
- **Deal Qualification Scoring** - Automated qualification reviews based on sales frameworks
- **Objection Reporting** - Monthly reports on top objections preventing deal closure
- **Universal Search** - Find insights across entire workspace and transcripts

### AI Coach
- **Sales Training** - Creates training materials from recorded calls and highlights
- **AI-Assisted Coaching** - Provides scorecards and feedback on rep performance
- **Video Library** - Secure organization of recordings and training assets

## Integrations

Supports 30+ integrations including Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Slack, Google Workspace, Linear, Jira, and more.
