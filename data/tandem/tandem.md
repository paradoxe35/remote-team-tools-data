## Overview

Tandem is a virtual office designed specifically for hybrid and remote teams that crave real-time connection and spontaneous collaboration. With just one click, you can see who's online, what they're working on, and jump into a chat or project.

## Features

- **Presence Awareness** - See who's online and available at a glance
- **Activity Visibility** - Check what team members are working on in real-time
- **One-Click Communication** - Instantly jump into conversations without scheduling
- **Screen Sharing** - Share your screen for quick collaboration and feedback
- **Spontaneous Collaboration** - Recreate the "tap on the shoulder" moments of office life
- **Team Coordination** - Keep distributed teams aligned and connected throughout the workday
- **Work Together, Wherever You Are** - Bridge the gap between remote and in-office team members

## Use Cases

- Quick questions and clarifications
- Pair programming and code reviews
- Design feedback and iteration
- Project coordination and status updates
- Building team relationships across distances
