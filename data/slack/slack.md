## Features

- Channel-based team communication (e.g. #project-name, #onboarding)
- Direct messaging and group chats
- File sharing and collaborative document editing
- Integrations with +2,200 apps (Office 365, Google Drive, etc.)
- Searchable message history
- Voice/video calls

## Pricing

- Free plan: Basic features for unlimited users
- Plus plan: $6.67/user/month (enhanced search, advanced permissions)
- Enterprise plan: Custom pricing for large teams

## Best For

Quick decisions, real-time collaboration, and keeping remote teams connected through centralized communication.
