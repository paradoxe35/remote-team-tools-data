## Overview

Twist is the only Slack alternative designed specifically for asynchronous teamwork. Made by Doist (creators of Todoist), Twist emphasizes "productivity over presence" with the tagline "Work async, not ASAP." The platform helps 71% of teams report calmer collaboration compared to real-time chat tools.

## Features

### Thread-Based Communication
- **Organized Threads** - Hyper-focused threads keep discussions on-topic and easy to follow
- **No Chat Chaos** - Ditch free-flowing Slack chats for structured, searchable conversations
- **Persistent Context** - Important information never gets buried in endless chat scrolls
- **Async-First Design** - Team members reply at their own pace without instant-response pressure

### Organization
- **Channels** - Structure by topic, project, or client for better team visibility
- **Inbox** - Centralized view of threads requiring attention for easy prioritization
- **Search** - Find past conversations and decisions quickly
- **No Presence Indicators** - Deliberately omits online status to encourage deep work

### Communication Features
- **Direct Messages** - One-to-one private communication with emoji and GIF support
- **Smart Notifications** - Intentional, non-intrusive alerts reduce anxiety
- **File Sharing** - Share documents and media within organized threads
- **Markdown Support** - Format messages for clarity

## Philosophy

Unlike Slack which is built for real-time, rapid collaboration, Twist focuses on deep, structured discussions—great for teams that prefer deep work over constant pings. By removing real-time pressure, teams can think before responding and maintain work-life balance.

## User Impact

- 71% of teams report calmer collaboration

- Better organization compared to traditional chat tools
