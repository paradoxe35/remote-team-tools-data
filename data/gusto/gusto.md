## Overview

Gusto is a cloud-based payroll, benefits, and HR platform designed to help small and mid-sized teams simplify their HR operations. It provides automated payroll processing, benefits administration, compliance management, and employee self-service capabilities.

## Key Features

- **Payroll Automation**: Automatically calculates payroll, deductions, and taxes
- **Tax Compliance**: Handles federal, state, and local tax calculations and filings
- **Time Tracking Integration**: Syncs with time tracking data to automate hours for payroll
- **Benefits Administration**: Manage health insurance, 401(k), and other benefits
- **Employee Directory**: Centralized employee information with self-service profiles
- **Time Off Management**: Track vacation, sick leave, and PTO with approval workflows
- **Contractor Management**: Manage and pay contractors separately from employees
- **Mobile App**: Employees can view payslips, update information, and track time on mobile
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Comprehensive reports on payroll costs and company metrics
- **Integrations**: Connects with accounting software, time tracking tools, and business systems
- **HR Documents**: Centralized storage for employee documents and onboarding materials

## Pricing

- **Core Payroll**: Starting at $39/month plus per-employee fees
- **Payroll Plus**: Includes benefits administration starting around $119-159/month
- **Full-Service Payroll**: Premium option with dedicated support and advanced features
- **Pay-As-You-Grow**: Pricing scales with company growth
- **Custom Enterprise**: Contact for custom quotes for larger organizations
