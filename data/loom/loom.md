## Overview

Loom is a video messaging and screen recording tool designed for asynchronous communication in remote teams. It enables quick video sharing that's faster than writing detailed instructions or explanations.

## Key Features

- **Screen Recording**: Capture your screen with webcam overlay
- **Quick Share**: Generate shareable links for easy distribution
- **Slack Integration**: Send Loom videos directly to Slack
- **Transcripts**: Automatic transcription of video content
- **Reactions**: Viewers can react to videos with emoji
- **Comments**: Add timestamps and comments to videos
- **Video Libraries**: Organize and manage recorded videos
- **Mobile Recording**: Record videos on iOS and Android
- **Privacy Controls**: Manage who can view and share videos
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Quick recording controls

## Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Basic video recording with limited storage
- **Starter**: $10/month per user, includes up to 100 GB storage and advanced sharing
- **Professional**: $20/month per user, adds team management and custom branding
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for large organizations with dedicated support and SSO
