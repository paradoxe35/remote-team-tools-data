## Overview

Loom is a video messaging platform that enables remote teams to communicate asynchronously through screen and camera recordings. Powered by AI, Loom automatically generates titles, summaries, chapters, and tasks from your videos, ensuring clear communication and reducing the need for synchronous meetings.

## Key Features

- **Screen & Camera Recording**: Record your screen, camera, or both simultaneously with high-quality capture
- **Multiple Recording Options**: Chrome extension, desktop app, or mobile app for recording from any device
- **AI Automatic Processing**: AI Suite adds context automatically including titles, summaries, chapters, and action items
- **Transcription & Chapters**: Automatic speech-to-text with searchable chapters for easy navigation
- **Interactive Playback**: Viewers can quickly consume and respond to video messages without watching the entire duration
- **Integrations**: Seamless integration with Slack, Google Drive, Notion, Jira, Confluence, and other work tools
- **Atlassian Integration**: Native integration with Jira and Confluence for engineering teams
- **Smart Search**: Search across all your recorded videos by content, transcript, or metadata
- **Recording Analytics**: Track video engagement and viewer interactions

## Impact

Loom customers recorded 93 million videos in 2025, reducing the need for an estimated 245 million meetings.
