## Overview

Culture Amp is the market-leading employee experience and people analytics platform that provides tools for organizations to measure, analyze, and improve employee engagement, company culture, and employee performance. With more than 1.1 billion data points collected from companies worldwide, Culture Amp delivers actionable insights backed by organizational psychology and people science.
