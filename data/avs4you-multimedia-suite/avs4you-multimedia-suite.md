## Overview

AVS4YOU Multimedia Suite is designed for remote teams needing to create professional-grade audio and video content. The suite includes tools for editing, converting, and repurposing multimedia for internal communication, training, and marketing purposes.

## Features

- Video editing and conversion
- Audio editing and conversion
- Batch processing for multiple files
- Windows-only platform
- Output format customization
