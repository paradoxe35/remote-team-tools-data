## Overview

Otter.ai is an AI-powered platform that automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings and audio. For distributed teams, it provides an accurate record of discussions, making it easier to stay informed and follow up on action items.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Transcription**: Real-time transcription of meetings with 99% accuracy
- **Meeting Summaries**: AI-generated summaries highlighting key points and decisions
- **Action Items**: Automatic identification and tracking of action items from meetings
- **Speaker Identification**: Identifies and labels different speakers in transcripts
- **Full Search**: Search through all meeting transcripts and notes
- **Highlights & Notes**: Manually highlight important sections and add custom notes
- **Integrations**: Works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and others
- **Collaboration Features**: Share transcripts and summaries with team members
- **Mobile App**: Capture notes on the go and access transcripts anywhere
- **Export Options**: Download transcripts as text, PDF, or video clips
- **Live Notes**: Review notes as the meeting happens or catch up later
- **Conversation Intelligence**: Analyze tone, sentiment, and meeting patterns
