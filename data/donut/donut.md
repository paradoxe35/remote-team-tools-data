## Overview

Donut is an employee experience platform that automates and scales people programs directly within Slack and Microsoft Teams. Since launching in 2016, Donut has made more than 18.5 million connections across 20,000+ companies, helping teams build stronger relationships and culture remotely.

## Features

### AI Onboarding (Donut Journeys)
- **Automated Workflows** - Launch new hire onboarding in minutes
- **Tasks, Messages, and Polls** - 4.8M+ automated interactions delivered
- **HRIS/ATS Integration** - Syncs with BambooHR, Greenhouse, Workday, and others
- **555,000+ New Hires Onboarded** - Proven at scale
- **Custom Buddy Programs** - Connect new employees with mentors

### Smart Introductions (Donut Channels)
- **AI-Matched Connections** - Random pairings for virtual coffee chats
- **Cross-Team Networking** - Strengthen internal networks across departments
- **18.5M Introductions Made** - Massive connection facilitation
- **Spontaneous Interactions** - Encourage organic relationship building
- **Flexible Pairing** - Coffee chats, lunch buddies, peer learning partners

### Recognition Program (Donut Shoutouts)
- **Peer-to-Peer Recognition** - Team members celebrate each other's wins
- **Points and Badges** - Gamified recognition system
- **Customizable Rewards** - Create meaningful reward programs
- **Culture Building** - Foster appreciation and positive team dynamics

### Watercooler Conversations
- **Asynchronous Engagement** - Fun conversation prompts posted automatically
- **Weekly Icebreakers** - Keep teams connected without meetings
- **Community Building** - Create virtual water cooler moments

### Video Facilitation (Gatheround by Donut)
- **Interactive Sessions** - Host engaging team meetings
- **AI-Supported Agendas** - Get help planning and facilitating
- **Structured Activities** - Guided team-building experiences
