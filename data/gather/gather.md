## Overview

Gather is a virtual office platform designed to help remote teams recreate the spontaneity and collaboration of physical office spaces in a digital environment. The platform uses customizable 2D spaces where team members can move around, interact, and collaborate naturally.

## Features

- **Virtual Office Spaces** - Customizable digital workspaces where teams can see who's available and interact spontaneously
- **Spatial Audio and Video** - Audio and video that gets louder as you get closer to colleagues, mimicking real-world interactions
- **Meeting Rooms** - Dedicated spaces for focused discussions and presentations
- **Screen Sharing** - Share your screen with nearby colleagues or in meeting rooms
- **Interactive Objects** - Embed whiteboards, videos, and other tools directly into your virtual space
- **Custom Spaces** - Design your office to match your team's culture and workflow
- **Integrations** - Connect with calendar, Slack, and other productivity tools
- **Event Hosting** - Host conferences, workshops, and social events in immersive virtual environments
