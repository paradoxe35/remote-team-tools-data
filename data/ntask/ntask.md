## Overview

nTask is a streamlined project management tool designed for teams that need to organize tasks and projects without unnecessary complexity. It offers visual scheduling through Gantt charts, customizable workflows, and collaborative features.

## Features

- **Gantt charts** for visualizing project timelines and dependencies
- **Checklists and custom fields** for detailed task organization
- **Workflow automation** to reduce manual processes
- **Team collaboration** with real-time updates and file sharing

## Limitations

- Interface can feel cluttered for users preferring minimalism
- Limited customization options for advanced workflows

## Pricing

- Pricing depends on team size, with plans starting at $10/user/month for basic features
- Custom pricing for larger teams or enterprise needs

## Ratings

- G2 rating: 4.4/5
