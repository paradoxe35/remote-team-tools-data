## Overview

Camtasia is a screen recording and video editing tool that simplifies content creation for tutorials, software demonstrations, and educational materials. It offers drag-and-drop timeline editing and built-in visual effects.

## Features

- One-click screen/video capture
- Built-in screen recorder with mouse tracking
- Timeline-based video editing
- Title templates and transitions
- Callout annotations and zoom/pan effects
