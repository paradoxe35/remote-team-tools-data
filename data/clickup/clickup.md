## Overview

ClickUp is an all-in-one project management platform designed to replace multiple tools. It offers extensive customization, powerful automation, and comprehensive features for managing tasks, projects, goals, and team collaboration.

## Key Features

- **Multiple Views**: List, board, box, timeline, calendar, and other custom views
- **Task Management**: Hierarchical task organization with subtasks and dependencies
- **Goals & Key Results**: Set and track organizational goals
- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracking and time estimates
- **Docs**: Integrated document creation and knowledge management
- **Automations**: No-code automation builder
- **Integrations**: 1,000+ integrations with popular tools
- **Custom Fields**: Create fields specific to team needs
- **Reporting**: Advanced dashboards and analytics
- **Mobile App**: Full functionality on iOS and Android

## Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Basic task and project management
- **Team**: $9 per user/month (billed annually), includes all views and automations
- **Business**: $19 per user/month, adds advanced reporting and integrations
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing with dedicated support and advanced security
