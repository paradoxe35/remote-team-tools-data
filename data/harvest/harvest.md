## Overview

Harvest helps professional teams track time, bill clients, optimize team time, and measure performance with confidence. Specifically designed for remote and distributed teams, Harvest makes it easy to log billable hours from anywhere and turn them into invoices in just 2 clicks.

## Features

### Time Tracking
- **Multi-Platform Tracking** - Track time via web, desktop (Mac and PC), and mobile apps (iOS and Android)
- **One-Click Timers** - Start and stop timers with a single click
- **Manual Time Entry** - Add time entries retroactively when needed
- **Unlimited Projects** - Track time across unlimited projects
- **Unlimited Team Members** - No limits on team size
- **Task-Level Tracking** - Break down time by specific tasks within projects

### Invoicing & Billing
- **2-Click Invoicing** - Generate and send invoices from tracked time instantly
- **Online Payments** - Accept payments via Stripe and PayPal
- **Multi-Currency Support** - Create invoices in any currency
- **Custom Invoices** - Customize invoices to fit each client's needs
- **Recurring Invoices** - Set up auto-generating invoices on your schedule
- **Direct Payment** - Clients pay directly from the invoice

### Team Management
- **Team Reports** - Track team productivity and utilization
- **Project Budgets** - Set budgets and track against them
- **Capacity Planning** - Understand team availability and workload
- **Performance Metrics** - Measure team performance with detailed analytics
- **Client Billing** - Track billable vs. non-billable time

### Integrations

Connect with popular tools for seamless workflows:
- Slack - Track time without leaving conversations
- Asana - Log time on Asana tasks
- Trello - Track time on Trello cards
- Google Calendar - See time entries in your calendar
- Basecamp, Jira, GitHub, and many more

### Remote Team Features
- **Work From Anywhere** - Access via web, desktop, or mobile
- **Client Portal** - Clients can view and approve time entries
- **Expense Tracking** - Log and bill for project expenses
- **Team Timesheet Approval** - Review and approve team timesheets
- **Time Tracking Reminders** - Automated reminders to log time
