## Overview

Productive is an all-in-one project management platform designed specifically for remote teams. It integrates task management, time tracking, budgeting, and reporting to reduce tool sprawl and increase visibility across distributed workflows.

## Key Features

- Task management with dependencies and workload visualization
- Real-time time tracking connected to budgets
- Project dashboards with performance metrics
- Cross-functional team workload balancing
- Time zone-aware scheduling and handoffs
- Document collaboration within tasks

## Pricing

- Free 14-day trial (no credit card required)
- Tiered pricing based on team size and features
- Custom enterprise pricing available

## Best For

- Remote-first agencies and consulting firms
- Teams needing unified task-time-budget visibility
- Distributed teams across multiple time zones
