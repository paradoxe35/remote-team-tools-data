## Overview

Forest is a focus and productivity application that gamifies work sessions by having users plant virtual trees that grow during focused work periods. The app discourages phone use by "killing" trees if the user leaves the app, creating an emotional connection that provides strong motivation for sustained focus. Featured in The New York Times, The Guardian, TechCrunch, and Business Insider.

## Features

- **Virtual Tree Planting** - Plant trees for 30-minute focus sessions that grow as you stay focused
- **Forest Building** - Build entire forests across multiple successful focus sessions
- **Multi-Scenario Support** - Customizable for office work, studying, socializing, and other activities
- **Real Tree Planting** - Partnership with Trees for the Future to plant real trees based on app usage
- **Browser Extensions** - Available for Firefox and Chrome to block distracting websites
- **Motivational Design** - Emotional connection to virtual trees encourages sustained focus
- **Social Features** - Compare forests with friends and compete for focus achievements
- **Customizable Timers** - Set custom focus session lengths

## Real-World Impact

Forest has facilitated the planting of 109,760+ real trees through partnerships with environmental organizations, giving users' focus sessions tangible environmental benefits.
