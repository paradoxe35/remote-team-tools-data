## Overview

Achievers is a recognition and rewards platform that helps remote teams maintain engagement through built-in tools for: 

- Real-time recognition in Slack, Teams, and Outlook
- Global reward marketplace (3M+ options)
- Employee connection tools (1:1 pairing, onboarding)
- Analytics for engagement tracking


## Key Features

- Embedded in 10+ communication platforms
- Reward fulfillment in ~190 countries
- Engagement analytics dashboard
- Multilingual 24/7 support
