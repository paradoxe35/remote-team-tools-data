## Overview

SoWork is an AI-powered cloud-based SaaS platform designed for distributed teams. Founded in 2019, it creates a shared digital workplace combining spatial collaboration, integrated chat, online meetings, workplace analytics, and AI-driven productivity tools with persistent presence.

## Features

- **Spatial Video and Audio** - Persistent avatars in customizable virtual office environments with spatial audio
- **Integrated Messaging** - Built-in chat functionality for text communication
- **Online Meetings** - Full meeting capabilities with screen sharing and annotation tools
- **AI-Generated Transcripts** - Automatic meeting transcripts, recordings, and summaries
- **AI Action Items** - Automatically extract action items from meetings
- **Searchable Meeting Libraries** - Easy reference to past meetings and discussions
- **Workplace Analytics** - Team visibility tools and productivity insights
- **Cross-Platform** - Available on web, iOS, and Android
- **Simplified Mode** - Optional streamlined interface for teams not ready for full virtual office experience
- **Customizable Environments** - Design your virtual office to match team culture
