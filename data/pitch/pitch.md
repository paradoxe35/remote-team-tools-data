## Overview

Pitch is the complete pitching platform that takes your design and delivery to the next level, keeping everyone on brand with templates, collaboration on slides, and sharing decks or entire deal rooms. It's a modern, collaborative presentation platform designed for fast-moving teams that need to create professional presentations quickly.

## Features

### Templates & Design
- **Designer-Quality Templates** - Vast library of professional templates
- **Intuitive Drag-and-Drop** - Easy-to-use editing interface
- **Brand Consistency** - Keep everyone on brand with custom templates
- **Smart Layouts** - Automatically adapting layouts for content
- **Professional Polish** - Beautiful designs without design skills
- **Custom Branding** - Add logos, colors, and fonts

### Real-Time Collaboration
- **Multiplayer Editing** - Teams edit presentations together simultaneously
- **Live Comments** - Discuss slides in real-time
- **Version History** - Track changes and restore previous versions
- **Team Workspaces** - Shared spaces for presentation libraries
- **Role-Based Permissions** - Control who can view and edit

### Presentation Sharing
- **Deal Rooms** - Share entire collections of presentations
- **Link Sharing** - Send presentations via secure links
- **Embedding** - Embed presentations in websites and apps
- **Offline Mode** - Present without internet connection
- **Export Options** - PDF, PowerPoint, and other formats

### Analytics & Insights
- **Viewer Engagement Tracking** - See who viewed your presentation and when
- **Slide-Level Analytics** - Understand which slides resonate
- **Time Spent Analysis** - Track how long viewers spend on each slide
- **Engagement Metrics** - Measure presentation effectiveness
- **CRM Integration** - Sync analytics with your CRM

### Integrations

Seamlessly connects with essential tools:
- Slack - Share and discuss presentations
- Figma - Import designs directly
- Google Workspace - Easy file management
- Dropbox - Cloud storage integration
- Unsplash - Access stock images
- Giphy - Add animated GIFs

### AI-Powered Features (2026)
- **AI Content Generation** - Generate slide content with AI
- **Automated Layout Suggestions** - Smart design assistance
- **Text Expansion** - AI helps expand or summarize content
- **Smart Templates** - AI-recommended templates based on content
