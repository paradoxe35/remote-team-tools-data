## Overview

Kickidler is an all-in-one remote team management platform combining time tracking, employee monitoring, and project management capabilities.

## Key Features

* Real-time screen monitoring and live sessions
* Detailed productivity reports and analytics
* Task assignment and deadline tracking
* Cross-platform support (Windows/macOS/Linux)
* Data loss prevention (DLP) capabilities
* Billable hours tracking and timesheet generation

## Pricing

Subscription-based pricing with tiered plans starting at $29/month for basic features (up to 10 users). Enterprise solutions available upon request.
