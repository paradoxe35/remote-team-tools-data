## Overview

Paycor is a unified HCM (Human Capital Management) platform designed to streamline payroll, HR, and employee management for mid-market and enterprise organizations. It eliminates disconnected systems by providing seamless data flow between payroll, time tracking, and HR modules.

## Key Features

- **Payroll Management**: Automated payroll processing with federal, state, and local tax compliance
- **Time & Attendance**: Track employee hours with integrations to payroll calculations
- **HR Management**: Employee records, onboarding workflows, and compliance management
- **Benefits Administration**: Manage health insurance, retirement plans, and other benefits
- **Talent Development**: Performance reviews, learning management, and career development tools
- **Employee Self-Service**: Portal for employees to access pay stubs, benefits, and personal information
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Dashboards and reports on payroll costs, turnover, and workforce metrics
- **Mobile App**: Managers and employees can access information and approve time/expenses on mobile
- **ACA Compliance**: Automated ACA tracking and reporting for employer mandate compliance
- **Tax Compliance**: Stay current with federal, state, and local tax regulation changes
- **Integrations**: Connects with accounting software, benefits providers, and business systems

## Pricing

- **Payroll Starter**: Starting around $49/month plus per-employee fees
- **Full HCM**: Bundled pricing combining payroll, HR, and benefits modules
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for large organizations with dedicated support
- **Implementation**: Additional costs for setup and data migration from legacy systems
