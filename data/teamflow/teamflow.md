## Overview

Teamflow is a virtual office platform designed to help remote teams replicate the spontaneity and connectivity of traditional office environments. The platform combines virtual co-working spaces, one-click meetings, and integrated app collaboration in a unified workspace.

## Features

- **Virtual Office Space** - Spontaneous co-working areas with whiteboards for impromptu problem-solving and collaboration
- **One-Click Meetings** - Instantly join conversations without scheduling or waiting in lobbies
- **Multi-Screen Sharing** - Share multiple screens simultaneously during meetings
- **Shared Cursors** - Collaborate in real-time with visible cursors for better coordination
- **App Integration** - Embed tools like Asana, Salesforce, Figma, and Google Docs directly into conversations
- **Meeting Documentation** - Store documents and apps within meeting spaces for centralized reference
- **Real-time Collaboration** - True multiplayer workspace with collaborative editing capabilities
- **Persistent Spaces** - Maintain context across sessions with persistent meeting rooms and workspaces
