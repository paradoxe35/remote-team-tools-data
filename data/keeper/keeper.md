## Overview

Keeper is an enterprise-grade password manager and secrets management platform built with zero-knowledge, zero-trust architecture. It's designed for organizations that need to securely manage and share credentials across distributed teams while maintaining enterprise-level security and compliance.

## Key Features

- **Zero-Knowledge Encryption**: End-to-end encryption where even Keeper can't access your data
- **Zero-Trust Architecture**: Assumes all connections are untrusted until authenticated
- **Secure Credential Sharing**: Share passwords, files, and secrets with team members securely
- **Enterprise Vault**: Centralized vault for team credentials and sensitive information
- **Role-Based Access Control**: Fine-grained permission management with administrative controls
- **Multi-Factor Authentication**: Support for various MFA methods including biometrics
- **Audit Logging**: Complete audit trails for compliance and security investigations
- **Secrets Manager**: Manage API keys, tokens, and secrets for development teams
- **DNS Failover & HA**: High availability and disaster recovery features
- **SSO Integration**: Integration with Azure AD, Okta, and other identity providers
- **Mobile Apps**: Secure credential access on iOS and Android devices
- **Advanced Reporting**: Security dashboards and compliance reporting
