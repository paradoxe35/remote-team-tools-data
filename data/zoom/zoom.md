## Overview
Zoom facilitates virtual meetings, webinars, and team collaboration with features like HD video, breakout rooms, and cloud recording.

## Features
- High-definition video and audio
- Screen sharing and annotation
- Meeting recording and transcription
- Virtual backgrounds and breakout rooms
- Integration with calendar apps and collaboration tools

## Pricing
- Free plan (up to 40 minutes/group meetings)
- Pro plan ($14.99/host/month) with extended meeting time
- Business and Enterprise plans (custom pricing)
