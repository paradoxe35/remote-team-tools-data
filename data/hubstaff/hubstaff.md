## Overview

Hubstaff is a time tracking and remote workforce management solution trusted by over 95,000 businesses globally. It provides comprehensive tools to record employee hours, track productivity, and manage distributed teams across multiple locations.

## Key Features

- **Automated Time Tracking**: Desktop, mobile, and web timers available across Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS
- **Chrome Extension**: Browser-based tracking for web-based work
- **Activity Monitoring**: Optional screenshots, app/URL usage tracking, and activity indicators
- **GPS Tracking**: Geofencing capabilities for field and job site workers
- **Automated Timesheets**: Automatic timesheet generation with editing and approval workflows
- **Detailed Reporting**: 20+ customizable reports providing insights into team productivity and time allocation
- **Scheduling**: Built-in shift scheduling and team availability management
- **Approvals**: Streamlined timesheet approval process for managers
- **Integrations**: 35+ integrations with project management tools (Asana, Monday.com, etc.), payroll systems, and other platforms
- **Privacy Options**: Configurable monitoring levels to balance tracking with team privacy concerns
- **Dashboard**: Real-time visibility into team activity and productivity metrics
