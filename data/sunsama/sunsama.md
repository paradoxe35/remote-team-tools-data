## Overview

Sunsama is a daily planning and focus tool designed specifically for remote teams. It helps individuals and teams organize their work priorities, stay focused throughout the day, and maintain clear communication about what everyone is working on.

## Key Features

- **Daily Planning**: Structured daily planning ritual to prioritize work and stay focused
- **Tool Integration**: Pulls tasks from Asana, Jira, Linear, Notion, and other task management tools
- **Team View**: See what teammates are working on and their daily priorities
- **Time Blocking**: Block time for focused work and breaks
- **Asynchronous Status**: Share daily progress without synchronous meetings
- **AI Assistant**: Smart recommendations for organizing and prioritizing tasks
- **Standup Alternative**: Async daily updates replace traditional standup meetings
- **Slack Integration**: Quick status updates via Slack
- **Calendar Sync**: Integrates with Google Calendar or Outlook for time management
- **Progress Tracking**: Track how much of daily plan was completed
- **Shared Views**: Team dashboard showing everyone's priorities and progress
- **Mobile Support**: Plan and review priorities on mobile devices
