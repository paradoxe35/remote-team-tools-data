## Overview

ScreenRec is a productivity tool designed for instant video messaging and screenshot sharing, enabling teams to clearly show what's on their screen to teammates or AI. With over 700,000 active users and 16 million screen recordings created, it's a popular choice for remote collaboration.

## Features

- **1-Click Screen Capture** - Record screen, microphone, audio, and webcam simultaneously with a single click
- **Annotated Screenshots** - Add comments and highlight important elements in screenshots
- **Instant Sharing** - Automatic shareable link generation for quick distribution
- **Privacy Controls** - Encrypted content with customizable access permissions
- **Analytics** - Track who viewed videos, when, and for how long
- **Content Management** - Tag files, create collections, and publish web-ready content
- **No Watermarks** - Free version includes no watermarks on recordings
- **Cross-Platform** - Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux

## Use Cases

- Software development and bug reporting
- Customer support demonstrations
- Design feedback and reviews
- eLearning and training materials
- Sales presentations
- Employee onboarding
- Project management updates
- Business communication

## Platform Availability

- Windows (10, 8, 7, Vista, XP)
- macOS (Apple Silicon and Intel)
- Linux (Ubuntu-based systems)

## Pricing

ScreenRec offers a free forever version with no credit card required. Paid plans are available with additional features.
