## Overview

Pumble is a business messaging alternative to Slack that lets your team communicate and see entire message history from the beginning. Unlike competitors like Slack that limit chat history to 90 days on free plans, Pumble offers unlimited chat history even on the free tier, making it ideal for small to mid-sized teams, remote teams, nonprofits, and startups seeking cost-effective communication.

## Features

### Free Plan Highlights
- **Unlimited Chat History** - Access all messages from day one, forever
- **Unlimited Users** - No limit on team size
- **Unlimited Channels** - Create as many channels as needed
- **Free Voice & Video Calls** - One-on-one calls at no cost
- **File Sharing** - Share files with your team
- **Search Functionality** - Find any message in your history

### Communication Features
- **Channels** - Organized conversations by topic or team
- **Direct Messages** - Private one-on-one or group conversations
- **Threads** - Keep discussions organized within channels
- **Mentions & Notifications** - @mention teammates and customize alerts
- **Emoji Reactions** - Quick responses to messages
- **File Attachments** - Share documents, images, and files

### Voice & Video
- **Voice Calls** - One-on-one voice calls (free)
- **Video Calls** - One-on-one video calls (free)
- **Group Meetings** - Multi-participant calls (Pro plan)
- **Screen Sharing** - Share your screen during calls (Pro plan)
- **Call Recording** - Record important conversations (Pro plan)

### Team Organization
- **User Groups** - Organize team members efficiently
- **Custom Statuses** - Show availability and what you're working on
- **Do Not Disturb** - Control when you receive notifications
- **Workspace Customization** - Tailor the interface to your needs

### Migration & Setup
- **Built-in Slack Import** - Migrate channels, messages, and files from Slack easily
- **Easy Onboarding** - Quick setup for new teams
- **Intuitive Interface** - Familiar layout for Slack users
- **No Learning Curve** - Start using immediately

## Pricing
