## Features

- Integration with Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Drive)
- Secure video meetings with up to 100 participants (free plan)
- Screen sharing and real-time document collaboration
- Extended meeting durations with paid plans
- Meeting recordings stored in Google Drive

## Pricing

- Free plan: 1-hour meetings, 100 participants
- Workplace Essentials Plan: $8/user/month (300-hour max, 150 participants, cloud storage)
