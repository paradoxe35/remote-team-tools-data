## Overview

Freshdesk is an AI-powered customer service solution that's easy to set up, simple to use, and built to boost agent productivity and deliver seamless customer experiences. Trusted by more than 60,000 customers worldwide, Freshdesk brings all customer conversations into a single, easily accessible location.

## Features

### Ticketing System
- **Multi-Channel Ticketing** - Converts customer inquiries from email, phone, chat, and social media into manageable tickets
- **Ticket Management** - Track, prioritize, and resolve tickets efficiently
- **Agent Collision Detection** - Prevents multiple agents from working on the same ticket
- **Categorization & Routing** - Automatic ticket categorization and routing to the right team or agent
- **Ticket Prioritization** - Automatically prioritize tickets based on urgency and impact

### Automation & Efficiency
- **Workflow Automation** - Automate repetitive tasks to save time
- **SLA Management** - Set SLA policies for response and resolution times
- **Auto-Reminders** - Automatically remind agents or escalate issues to managers when SLAs aren't met
- **Canned Responses** - Pre-written responses for common questions

### AI Capabilities
- **Freddy AI Copilot** - Drafts intelligent replies and summarizes tickets for faster context ($29/agent/month add-on)
- **AI-Powered Suggestions** - Helps agents respond more effectively
- **Ticket Summarization** - Quickly understand ticket context with AI summaries

### Communication & Collaboration
- **Multi-Channel Support** - Email, phone, chat, social media, and web in one platform
- **Team Collaboration** - Internal notes and agent collaboration features
- **Customer Portal** - Self-service portal for customers to find answers

### Reporting & Analytics
- **Pre-Built Reports** - Track team performance and customer satisfaction
- **Custom Dashboards** - Create dashboards tailored to your metrics
- **Performance Insights** - Monitor agent productivity and ticket resolution times

### Knowledge Base
- **Self-Service Portal** - Help customers find answers without contacting support
- **Article Management** - Create and organize help articles
- **Search Functionality** - Easy-to-use search for knowledge base articles
