## Overview

Sembly AI is an AI-powered meeting assistant designed to enhance collaboration and productivity by automating the capture, transcription, and summarization of meetings. It helps teams turn meeting conversations into actionable insights and organized documentation.

## Key Features

- **Automated Transcription**: Accurate transcription for all meetings with speaker identification and multilingual support (45+ languages)
- **AI Summarization**: Intelligent summaries that distill meeting content into key points and decisions
- **Action Item Extraction**: Automatic identification and organization of follow-up tasks and decisions
- **Collections**: Group related discussions and organize meeting data into coherent collections
- **Sharing & Collaboration**: Share AI-generated notes and transcriptions with colleagues and managers
- **Microsoft Teams Integration**: Automatically sends structured notes and transcriptions directly to Teams channels
- **Discussion Analytics**: AI-powered chat to ask questions and gain insights about meeting content
- **Workflow Automation**: Native integrations and Zapier support for syncing meeting details across platforms
- **Pre-Built Templates**: Workflow templates for common integration scenarios without coding
- **Multilingual Support**: Handles meetings conducted in 45+ languages with accurate transcription
- **Speaker Recognition**: Identifies and assigns authorship regardless of background noise

## Security & Compliance

- **Data Protection**: GDPR, EU-USDPF, FERPA, and PCI DSS compliant
- **Healthcare**: HIPAA audit completed and SOC 2 Type II certified
- **Enterprise**: Microsoft 365 Certification for enterprise use
- **Privacy**: Enterprise-grade encryption and strict access controls

Native integrations with Microsoft Teams and Zapier for connecting with 500+ other applications
