## Overview
ActiveCollab helps teams manage projects, track time, and communicate with clients in a single platform. It streamlines workflows with customizable task lists, Gantt charts, and automated reminders.

## Features
- Task and project management
- Time tracking with invoicing
- Client feedback and approvals
- Custom workflows and automation
- Gantt charts and timeline views

## Pricing
- Free plan for 1 user
- Starter plan ($19/user/month)
- Professional plan ($29/user/month)
- Enterprise plans (custom pricing)
