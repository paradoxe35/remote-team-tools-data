## Overview

Rippling is an all-in-one workforce management platform designed to eliminate disconnected systems and automate HR, payroll, benefits, and IT operations. It's built for companies that want a single system of record for all workforce data.

## Key Features

- **Unified Platform**: Integrates HR, payroll, benefits, IT, and finance in one system
- **Payroll Automation**: Handles federal, state, and local tax compliance across 200+ countries
- **Benefits Management**: Streamline health insurance, 401(k), and other benefits enrollment
- **Time & Attendance**: Track time, manage schedules, and integrate with payroll
- **IT Management**: Device provisioning, access management, and employee offboarding
- **Employee Directory**: Single source of truth for all employee information
- **Travel & Expenses**: Manage business travel, mileage, and expense reimbursements
- **Talent Management**: Performance reviews, goal tracking, and career development
- **Compliance & Audit**: Automated compliance tracking and audit reporting
- **Global Payroll**: Support for payroll in 200+ countries with local compliance
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)**: Centralized identity management and access controls
- **API & Integrations**: Extensive integrations with business applications
