## Overview

GoToMeeting is a virtual meeting solution designed for SMBs, offering real-time video conferencing, screen sharing, and AI-powered meeting summaries.

## Features
- High-definition video meetings
- Screen sharing and annotation
- AI-generated meeting summaries
- Personal meeting rooms
- Integration with calendar apps

## Pricing
- **Basic**: $12/user/month
- **Premium**: $16/user/month
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

## Limitations
- Requires annual billing
- Limited free plan functionality

## Best For
Small to medium-sized businesses needing reliable virtual meetings.
