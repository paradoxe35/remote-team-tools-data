## Overview

Founded in 2012 in Barcelona, Typeform is an AI-powered engagement platform that enables users to build interactive forms, surveys, quizzes, and polls at the drop of a prompt. Used by 95% of Fortune 500 companies, Typeform helps organizations collect 3.5x more data while providing 96% better brand experiences.

## Features

### Typeform AI
- **AI Form Generation** - Build and edit forms using natural language prompts
- **Automated Structure** - AI structures and designs forms based on your command
- **Question Generation** - AI can generate questions and refine wording
- **Logic Assistance** - Help structuring complex conditional logic

### Form Creation & Design
- **Multiple Form Types** - Forms, surveys, quizzes, polls, tests, landing pages, and application forms
- **Brand Kits** - Instantly apply logos, colors, and typography
- **Conditional Logic** - Shape each person's journey based on their answers
- **Video Engagement** - Create interactive video forms
- **No Coding Required** - User-friendly interface for all skill levels

### Data Collection & Automation
- **Contacts & Automations** - Automatic data segmentation and follow-up email triggers
- **Analytics & Reporting** - Data insights and visualization tools
- **Integration Hub** - Connect to 100+ business applications including Slack, Zapier, HubSpot, and Stripe
- **Admin & Security** - Enterprise-grade data protection features
