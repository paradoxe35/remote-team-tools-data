## Overview

ONLYOFFICE DocSpace is a room-based workspace that enables teams to collaborate on documents, spreadsheets, and presentations with granular access controls. Teams can manage multiple workspaces with custom permission levels while using AI-powered tools for content analysis and automation.

## Features

- Real-time collaborative editing
- Document version history and access control
- AI agents for content summarization and idea generation
- Chat, voice and video calls in collaboration rooms
- Public and private project workspaces

## Pricing

- Free plan for basic document collaboration
- Professional plans starting at $10/user/month
- Enterprise options with custom licenses
