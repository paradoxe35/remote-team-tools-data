## Overview

Motion is an AI-powered productivity platform that merges task management with intelligent time blocking. It automatically schedules tasks based on priority and availability, offering dynamic rescheduling and prioritization.

## Key Features

- AI task scheduling and time blocking
- Integrations with Google Calendar and Outlook
- Automatic rescheduling based on new tasks
- Personal and team-level planning views
- Priority-based task recommendations

## Pricing

Free plan for individuals. Paid plans start at $9.99/user/month for advanced features and team collaboration.
