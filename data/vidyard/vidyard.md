## Overview

Vidyard is an AI-powered video selling platform designed for virtual sales and marketing teams. Used by over 100,000 teams including Citrix, LinkedIn, Microsoft, and Salesforce, Vidyard enables businesses to increase leads, accelerate pipelines, and delight customers through personalized video messaging and engagement tools.

## Core Products

1. **Video Messages** - Send personalized videos to build customer relationships
2. **Video Agent** - AI-powered tool that automatically sends personalized videos with agentic AI
3. **AI Avatars** - Generate personalized AI videos at scale without recording
4. **Vidyard Hosting** - Centralized platform to manage and track business videos

## Key Features

- **Personalized Video Creation** - Create and send custom video messages at scale
- **Video Engagement Analytics** - Detailed viewer insights and engagement metrics
- **51+ Platform Integrations** - Connect with Salesforce, HubSpot, Gong, LinkedIn, Gmail, and more
- **Compliance Tools** - Ensure messaging consistency and brand compliance
- **AI Script Generation** - Generate video scripts with AI assistance
- **Free Screen Recorder** - Built-in screen recording tool
- **Video Hosting** - Centralized video management and distribution
- **Team Collaboration** - Share video libraries and best practices across teams
