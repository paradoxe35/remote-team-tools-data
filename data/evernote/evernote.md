## Overview
Evernote helps teams organize tasks, notes, and ideas in a centralized platform. It supports real-time collaboration, document sharing, and cross-device synchronization.

## Features
- Note-taking with text, images, and voice
- Task lists and reminders
- Cloud storage and syncing
- Document scanning and OCR
- Team collaboration and shared notebooks

## Pricing
- Free plan with basic features
- Premium plan ($7.99/month) with advanced organization tools
- Business plan (custom pricing)
