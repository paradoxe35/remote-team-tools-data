## Overview
Whimsical provides teams with a digital whiteboard for collaborative brainstorming, wireframing, and organizing ideas. It supports real-time editing and integrates with project management tools.

## Features
- Virtual sticky notes and whiteboards
- Flowchart and diagram creation
- Real-time collaboration with team members
- Integration with Asana, Trello, and Slack
- Commenting and @mentions

## Pricing
- Free plan (limited storage and features)
- Pro plan ($12/user/month)
- Team plans (custom pricing)
